about us

AK INSTITUTE of engineering a premier private-funded institution for higher technical education in India. Consistently ranked amongst the top 5 engineering institutes in India over past several decades. AK INSTITUTE offers courses in sciences, engineering, humanities and management. At AK INSTITUTE, there is a strong emphasis on research in all our academic programs and we also have several specialized centers for research into various focus areas. Many of our alumni are leaders in academia and industry around the globe. The sprawling AK INSTITUTE campus is located in the industrial city of Kanpur, only a few hours away from New Delhi, the Capital of India.

our placement partners

AK INSTITUTE has ended Phase-1 of the placement drive for the batch 2021-22. The institute witnessed 150% jump in the international offers and has received 47 international Job offers as compared to 19 last year. During the 2022 placement drive, the highest international salary package of INR 2.3 CPA & the highest domestic salary package of INR 1.2 CPA were offered. The average salary stood at INR 28.7 LPA AK INSTITUTE's MBA class of 2020-22 has achieved 100% placement rate, with all 55 students who participated in the placement process landing jobs in addition to the 11 pre-placement offers/interviews (PPO / PPI). During the global economic downturn, the MBA programme saw a year-over-year (YoY) average CTC rise of 22.54 percent. AK INSTITUTE's MBA programme have always had among of the highest fee-to-cost ratios, but this year the programme outdid itself, with a 1:9 average fee-to-cost ratio, the most in the country. AK INSTITUTE concluded its 2020-21 placement drive with 226 placement offers wherein Graviton Trading picked 3 students for a package worth INR. 82 LPA and QuantBox offered a package of INR. 80 LPA to one student. Google and Microsoft also offered some attractive packages for domestic profiles at the institute. By the fourth day since the inauguration of its 2020-21 placement drive, the institute managed to bag a whopping INR. 1.47 Crore package by an MNC. Even amidst the pandemic, the institute hosted over 230 recruiters. The institute concluded the First Phase of the 2020-21 placement drive, with 821 offers out of 709 were accepted by the students. The entire placement process was conducted online. During the 2019-20 placement drive, the AK INSTITUTE had witnessed a 93.17% placement record. The highest CTC had stood at INR. 1.54 CPA. However, under the MBA program, the highest CTC had stood at INR. 20 LPA.
